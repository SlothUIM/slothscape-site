Features

Core content is built from Old School RuneScape’s r190-era assets, mechanics, and data. Rates and QoL are tuned for SlothScape’s chill pace without pay‑to‑win.

Core Engine

  • OSRS r190 cache: items, NPCs, maps, object defs, sequences, and varbits from the r190 data set.
  • Accurate combat formulas: OSRS-style attack rolls, max hits, prayer multipliers, and tick timing.
  • Pathing & interactions: region clipping, projectile logic, doors/ladder handling, and multi-combat rules.
  • Drop tables: boss tables and uniques aligned with r190, with broadcast announcements for notable drops.

Bossing & Raids (r190)

Chambers of Xeric (CoX)

Scalable raids with rotation, tightrope, guardians, Vasa/Vespula/Vanguard, and Olm phases. Unique rewards include Twisted Bow, Kodai, and ancestral kit.

Theatre of Blood (ToB)

Verzik’s theatre with Nylo waves, Bloat pathing, Sotetseg maze, Xarpus exhumed, and proper purple/white chest rules. Scythe, Ghrazi, and Justiciar sets.

The Nightmare of Ashihama

Totems, parasites, husks, sleepwalkers, and phase‑based tiles. Inquisitor’s set and mace on the unique table.

Gauntlet & Corrupted

Resource routing, weapon frame upgrades, Hunllef stomp/prayer cycles. Crystal armor seeds and weapon seeds available.

Zalcano

Mining/Smithing DPS loop, demonic runes, boulder mechanics, and crystal shard rewards consistent with r190.

Alchemical Hydra

Phase rotations, fountain resets, and poison pools. Hydra leather/Claw drops per r190 rates.

Other bosses

Zulrah, Vorkath, Cerberus, Abyssal Sire, Kraken, Skotizo, Kalphite Queen.

Wilderness (pre‑rework set)

Venenatis, Callisto, Vet’ion, Chaos Elemental/Fanatic, Scorpia with multi/singles rules and teleblock/skull behavior.

Skilling & World

Kebos Lowlands

Farming Guild contracts, Mount Karuulm, Konar tasks, Aerial Fishing (Molch), and Alchemical Hydra’s lair.

Prifddinas

Song of the Elves city, crystal shard economy, Zalcano, Gauntlet lobby, and crystal tools.

Fossil Island

Birdhouses, Herbiboar, underwater thieving/agility, Volcanic mine access.

Zeah/Great Kourend

House favor regions, Sand Crabs, Lovakengj blast mine, Arceuus essence.

Gathering & production

Motherlode Mine, Wintertodt, Rooftop agility, Rune crafting altars, Blast Furnace, and Charter crafting routes.

Slayer System

Konar/Nieve/Steve/Duradel tasks, unlocks, superior encounters, and boss tasks where applicable.

Minigames & Challenges

  • Barrows: set roll logic, chest RNG, and tunnel rotations.
  • Pest Control: portals, shields, brawler pathing, and point rewards.
  • TzHaar challenges: Fight Caves with Jad waves and Inferno for end‑game practice.
  • Clue scrolls: riddle, emote, and coordinate steps with dug locations and stash units where applicable.

Quality of Life

  • Modern client: GPU, UI scaling, instance timers, loot beams, tile indicators, and camera unlocks.
  • Cosmetic‑only perks: supporter badges, titles, and hub auras that never affect power.
  • Trade & safety: price hints on noted uniques, dupe protections, and anti‑scam confirmations.
  • Social systems: clan chat, group instance keys, and boss kill‑count broadcasts.
